ARIANNA to Visit the EU Cyber Resilience Act Conference in Brussels

Published March 9, 2026 · Estimated read time: 2 minutes

Back to resources

The ARIANNA team will visit the EU Cyber Resilience Act Conference in Brussels to meet organizations preparing for upcoming product cybersecurity requirements.

What to expect at our booth

At our booth, we will demonstrate ARIANNA with a specific focus on the operational relationship between vulnerability management and CRA readiness.

  • How vulnerability workflows support CRA-related obligations over time.
  • How teams can connect SBOM, triage, remediation, and evidence in one process.
  • How to reduce manual compliance effort through structured, audit-ready outputs.

Conference speaker session

ARIANNA CTO Massimo Ratti will speak at the conference about vulnerability management and its role in building practical, repeatable compliance operations.

If you are attending the conference, visit us in Brussels to see the platform in action and discuss your product-security and CRA implementation priorities.
