First Official ARIANNA Partner Announced: Security Pattern

Edition: March 2026

Partner Update

We are proud to announce that Security Pattern, the mother company behind ARIANNA, has become the first official ARIANNA partner, offering a comprehensive suite of services to support customers in adopting and operationalizing vulnerability management at scale.

With years of expertise in cybersecurity for embedded and connected systems, Security Pattern brings deep technical knowledge and proven methodologies to the ARIANNA ecosystem. As the founding organization behind the platform, they are uniquely positioned to help customers unlock its full potential.

What Security Pattern brings to ARIANNA customers

  • SBOM and vulnerability management enablement: support for implementing structured vulnerability management processes, including SBOM creation, vulnerability monitoring, and remediation workflows powered by ARIANNA.
  • Threat modeling and risk assessment: identification and prioritization of security risks early in the design and development lifecycle, enabling proactive mitigation strategies.
  • Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments: in-depth testing of devices and systems to uncover real-world vulnerabilities and validate security posture.
  • Compliance and regulatory readiness: guidance on meeting key standards and regulations such as IEC 62443, ISO/SAE 21434, MDR, RED DA, and the Cyber Resilience Act.
  • IoT security architecture and secure development: design and implementation of secure architectures, including secure boot, firmware protection, key management, and communication security.
  • Cybersecurity training and capability building: tailored training programs to upskill engineering and security teams on embedded security, vulnerability management, and secure development practices.

Why this matters

Security Pattern's official partnership marks an important milestone for the ARIANNA ecosystem. It ensures that customers not only gain access to a powerful vulnerability management platform, but also to expert services that drive real-world adoption, compliance, and impact.

Together, ARIANNA and Security Pattern provide a complete solution, from device-level visibility and vulnerability intelligence to hands-on implementation and regulatory readiness.
